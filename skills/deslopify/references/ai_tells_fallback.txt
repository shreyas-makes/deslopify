Fallback list of AI-writing tells (use when offline or fetch fails).

Phrases to avoid:
- "Here's the thing"
- "Let that sink in"
- "The uncomfortable truth is"
- "This matters because"
- "So what's the answer?"
- "Let me be clear"
- "I'm going to be honest"
- "The real unlock is"
- "Full stop"

Structures to avoid:
- "Not because X. Because Y."
- "[Noun]. That's it. That's the [thing]."
- "And that's okay."
- Repeated dramatic fragment sentences
- Question followed by immediate answer

Habits to avoid:
- Tripling lists by default (three examples every time)
- Starting paragraphs with "So" or "Look"
- Ending every section with a punchy one-liner
